Slovak and Relevant Film Festivals for Students

Information about festivals listed on this page could be old and unupdated, so I recommend you check each one by yourself.

  • Áčko

    A major showcase and competition for student films from Slovakia and surrounding countries.

    Slovakia Student

  • Cineama

    A national competition for amateur and non-professional filmmakers of all ages.

    Slovakia Amateur

  • Medart

    International student film festival organized by the Catholic University in Ružomberok.

    Slovakia Student International

  • Zlatá klapka

    A competition for young creators in audiovisual production, journalism, and media.

    Slovakia Youth

  • Mobilfest

    An international festival for short films shot exclusively on mobile phones.

    Slovakia International Mobile

  • České vize

    The top national competition for amateur and student filmmaking in the Czech Republic.

    Czechia Student Amateur

  • Animest

    A major international animation festival based in Bucharest, Romania, with various competitions.

    International Animation

  • Pražský filmový kufor (PFK)

    An international film festival for children and youth, focusing on works made by young creators.

    Czechia Youth International

  • Art Film Fest (AFF)

    One of Slovakia's largest film festivals, featuring an international competition for short films.

    Slovakia International Professional

  • International Film Fest Košice

    An international festival with a focus on short films across various genres.

    Slovakia International Short Film

  • Anča Fest

    International festival focused exclusively on animated film, with a major student competition.

    Slovakia International Animation Student

  • Úsvit

    A national award for student design, which includes categories for animation and audiovisual work.

    Slovakia Student

  • Trikfilm

    An international festival of animated films created by children and youth.

    Czechia International Animation Youth

  • IFF Ekotopfilm

    An international film festival dedicated to ecology, environment, and sustainable development.

    Slovakia International Environment

  • AZYL SHORTS

    A competition for very short films (1 and 5 minutes) and music videos from around the world.

    Slovakia International Short Film

  • Kamera

    A prestigious biennial award from the Association of Slovak Cinematographers for outstanding camera work.

    Slovakia Professional

  • Pocity Film Festival

    A film festival in Prešov that includes a competitive section for Slovak short films.

    Slovakia Short Film

  • Amfo

    The top national competition for amateur photographers, organized by the National Culture Centre.

    Slovakia Amateur Photography
